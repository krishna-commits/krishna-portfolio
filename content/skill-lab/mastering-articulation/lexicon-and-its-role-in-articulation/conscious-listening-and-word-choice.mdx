Active listening is crucial for understanding how words are used effectively in conversation or speech. 
By listening to articulate speakers and consciously focusing on their word choices, sentence structures,

## How it works:
By listening to highly articulate speakers, one can observe how they structure thoughts, vary sentence 
length, and use rhetorical devices to enhance communication. This helps internalize patterns of effective 
speech, making it easier to replicate these techniques in personal communication. This process of imitation 
and adaptation is crucial for developing a more refined and polished speaking style.
