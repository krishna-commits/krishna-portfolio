---

These devices amplify your message by stretching the meaning or adding embellishments, making your point more pronounced. They often introduce humor or drama to your speech, thus drawing the audience in further. Key devices in this category are:

## Amplification 
Adding detail to a statement to emphasize a point and make it more elaborate.
## Hyperbole
Exaggeration for dramatic or humorous effect.
## Personification
Attributing human qualities to inanimate objects or abstract concepts to make them more relatable and memorable.
