### Getting the detail of podman images 
```bash 

[sanjeeb@server ~]$ podman image inspect registry.redhat.io/rhel9/httpd-24:latest 

# To view in a page 
[sanjeeb@server ~]$ podman image inspect registry.redhat.io/rhel9/httpd-24:latest | less

```
- Important terms in inspect are `ExposedPorts` and `WorkingDir`.
