### Switching to root user
```bash
[sanjeeb@server ~]$ whoami 
sanjeeb
[sanjeeb@server ~]$ su -
Password: 
Last login: Sat Apr  6 15:31:02 +0545 2024 on pts/0
[root@server ~]# 
```

### Switching to a user
```bash
[root@server ~]# whoami 
root
[root@server ~]# su - sanjeeb
Last login: Sat Apr  6 15:38:46 +0545 2024 on tty2
Have a nice day!
lOGIN FROM SANJEEB!!
[sanjeeb@server ~]$ whoami
sanjeeb
```
