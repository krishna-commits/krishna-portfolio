## Networking for cloud-native and hybrid systems

Applied notes on **network design and operations** for DevSecOps and platform engineering: TCP/IP fundamentals, load balancers, firewall rules, WAF placement, VPN/peering concepts, and troubleshooting flows used in AWS, Azure, and on-prem hybrid setups.

### Topics covered

| Area | Focus |
|------|--------|
| **Fundamentals** | OSI/TCP-IP, DNS, TLS termination |
| **Security** | Firewalls, WAF, segmentation, least-privilege paths |
| **Cloud patterns** | VPC/VNet design, LB tiers, health checks |
| **Operations** | Juniper/router notes, OSPF basics, incident triage |

Part of the Operations & Reliability pillar  pairs with Linux, database, and observability tracks for end-to-end production readiness.
