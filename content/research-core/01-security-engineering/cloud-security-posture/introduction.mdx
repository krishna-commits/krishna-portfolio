## Research objective

Organizations operating multi-cloud environments struggle with **configuration drift** and inconsistent guardrails. This series documents CSPM patterns: detect → prioritize → remediate → prove compliance.

### Frameworks mapped

- CIS Benchmarks (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- SOC 2 Type II control families
- ISO 27001 Annex A technical controls
