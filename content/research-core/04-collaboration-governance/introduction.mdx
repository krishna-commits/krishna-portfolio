# Collaboration & Governance

Effective security and platform work depends on **how teams coordinate**. This pillar documents tooling and rituals that keep distributed engineers aligned.

## Research tracks

| Track | Focus |
|-------|--------|
| Communication | Slack, Jira, Confluence, Teams, Zoom integrations |
| Engineering Governance | RFCs, ticket hygiene, on-call communication |

## Governance topics

- Change advisory for production releases — see [RFC template](/research-core/04-collaboration-governance/engineering-governance/Chapter-1/rfc-design-review-template)
- On-call and incident comms norms — [On-Call Comms Playbook](/research-core/04-collaboration-governance/engineering-governance/Chapter-1/on-call-comms-playbook)
