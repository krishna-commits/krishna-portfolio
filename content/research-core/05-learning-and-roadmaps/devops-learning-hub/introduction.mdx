## About this track

The DevOps Learning Hub is the **starting point** for structured learning on this site. It replaces scattered bookmark lists with phased plans you can execute week by week.

### Chapters

| Chapter | Focus |
|---------|--------|
| **1 — Orientation & Roadmaps** | Foundation → senior paths; DevSecOps-weighted sequencing |
| **2 — Tools & Cloud Depth** | Tooling layers, provider study plans, hands-on labs |
| **3 — Exams & Interviews** | Certification order, scenario questions, portfolio proof |

### Philosophy

- **Depth over breadth** — one cloud, one CI system, one observability stack until proficient.
- **Security is not a final step** — scanning, secrets, and policy appear from Phase 1 onward.
- **Prove with artifacts** — pipelines, Terraform modules, and runbooks beat passive video watching.
