# Senior Platform Gap Checklist

If you already run production systems, skip beginner tutorials. Use this **self-audit** quarterly. Score each row: **0** = not in place, **1** = partial, **2** = automated and tested.

## Scoring guide

| Total score (max 52) | Interpretation |
|------------------------|----------------|
| 40–52 | Staff-ready platform posture; focus on mentoring and strategy |
| 26–39 | Strong operator; close gaps in lowest-scoring section first |
| &lt; 26 | Prioritize automation and security gates before new tool adoption |

---

## 90-day improvement plan

1. **Week 1–2:** Score honestly with your team lead; pick **two** rows at 0.
2. **Week 3–8:** Ship one automation per gap (pipeline rule, policy, or runbook).
3. **Week 9–12:** Tabletop incident; document evidence for compliance or promotion packet.

---

## Interview alignment

Senior interviews rarely ask syntax. Prepare **stories** for:

- A production incident you led and what changed afterward
- How you introduced scanning without blocking all releases
- A Terraform or K8s migration that reduced risk
- Trade-off between speed and compliance (with numbers)

See [Interview Preparation Playbook](/research-core/05-learning-and-roadmaps/devops-learning-hub/Chapter-3/interview-preparation-playbook).
